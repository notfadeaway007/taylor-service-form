Coverage Summary — Read Before Signing

✓ What's Covered

  1. Any inoperative electrical switch or control
  2. Any inoperative motor or compressor
  3. Any inoperative freezing cylinder, condenser or evaporator
  4. Any inoperative direct drive or gear box including couplings and pulleys
  5. Any inoperative fan motor, fan blade or blower assembly
  6. Any inoperative drive shaft, beater assembly, freezer door, draw valve, or torque rotor assembly
  7. Any refrigerant leak
  8. Any inoperative spinner motor or component thereof
  9. Any inoperative pump drive assembly
  10. Any inoperative rear shell bearing including bearing nut and lock washer (limited to one bearing per year per freezing cylinder)

✗ Not Covered (Normal Wear Items)

  • Check Bands
  • O-Rings
  • Door Gaskets
  • Front Bearings
  • Guide Bearings
  • Scraper Blades
  • Vinyl Mix Hoses
  • Plastic Pump Parts (after year one)
  • Design Caps / Restrictor Caps
  • Torque Arms
  • Cup Seals / Lip Seals
  • Belts
  • Dirty Condensers
  • Cleaning Supplies / Lubricant
  • Seasonal shutdown / startup
  • Misuse, negligence, acts of God

Limitations on Service Liability: The provisions of this contract do not apply to seasonal shutdown or start ups, normal maintenance or normal wear and deterioration, nor to any freezer or parts thereof that have been subject to misuse, negligence, fire, theft, vandalism, wind, rain, water, lightning, high water, earthquake, any natural disaster, act of God, or accident or have been repaired or altered in any way so as, in the judgment of the Manufacturer, to adversely affect performance. This contract will not cover return trips or waiting time necessitated by the owner/operator's unwillingness to allow the mechanic to begin working once the technician arrives. It is very important that the owner/operator be well versed in the operation and daily cleaning and maintenance of the machine as this contract will not provide coverage for breakdowns which are the direct result of misuse, abuse, or neglect. Under the terms of this contract, any part that requires replacement will be replaced with a Taylor Company original equipment part by a Taylor Distributor or their authorized service agent, during their normal servicing hours.
